# Voice Sample — Energy, the Cosmos, and Getting Back to Yourself

*On you as energy in the cosmos:*

"I would further define us — you and me — as energies in this cosmos. If you go with the flow of nature, and follow its laws, you will get what you want, whatever your original 14–15-year-old self desired. If you're running on someone's pathway, you're achieving someone's goal. Where the hell have your goals gone? A clear vision, without much of a filter, is needed."

*On simplicity and getting rid of what's in the way:*

"To be simple, pure, greed and lust-free. To you, success is a global mission, a global mission to help people strengthen themselves eternally. They need to understand some basics to reach eternity. But before you dream of eternity, you must first die. I would say your vices have to go and plainly die, never to come back. Your vices are induced by your confused and corrupted morals. Just the basics — I don't talk about much."
