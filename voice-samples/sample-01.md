# Voice Sample — Flow, Rhythm, and Time

*On how patterns compound:*

"Because they have figured out their flows, the flows are about using the same time doing the thing they really enjoy which continues the flow to the same next day and that flow somehow keeps going. That is which usually makes a person grow. There's no luck in flows, the flows makes and breaks you. So it's important to have a right set of flows. Flows are inspired by subconscious mind therefore it matters which content you consume, which TV shows you watch."

*On shortcuts:*

"One thing I have learnt so far in my life is shortcuts never work, instead what works is the actual WORK. Wisdom comes with time, knowledge comes with practice and power comes with discipline and consistency, but most important is how you understand it from the core, from the basics — so you can design your own journey with that basics like you know all chords on the guitar but it's your fingers and your practice that creates the tune and later you put that tune into your music masterpiece."
